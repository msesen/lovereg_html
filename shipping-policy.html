Shipping Policy

SHIPPING TERMS

Order and Shipping Confirmation

You will receive an email confirmation once your order has been successfully placed, it will include your order number and shipping method. Please make sure your shipping address is correct as we are unable to redirect goods once they are on route to you. Any incorrect address details will often result in the parcel being returned to the sender; in which case you will be charged for re-delivery. The Little Leopard Co. will not be responsible for any misplaced or unclaimed parcels by the courier company.

Delivery Timing and Dispatchment

  • Orders will be dispatched within 2 working days unless the gift is for an occasion further in the future.
  • Our standard delivery is Royal Mail Second Class delivery which may take 3 - 5 working days delivery from your placement of order.
  • We may offer Royal Mail First Class plus DPD Next Day Delivery for customers who need their orders quicker.
  • International shipping is available, and rates are calculated at checkout depending on weight of the item and destination.
  • If you are buying products during promotional periods, it may take a little longer for our products to be delivered to you.
  • We are not responsible for delays outside our control. If delivery of the products to you is delayed by an event outside our control (e.g. because of postal/courier delays, logistics or bad weather), we’ll let you know as soon as possible.

Delivery Fee

All Customers are required to pay the delivery/shipping fee for any orders they place.

Where We Ship

We currently ship directly within the United Kingdom and may deliver outside the country with extra shipping charges.

TAXES/CUSTOMS/VAT

International deliveries outside the United Kingdom may be subject to applicable taxes, VAT’s and duties which are to be paid by the Customer.

You are required to check with your local tax official to see what import duties and/or VAT you are required to pay in your place of residence.
