What is a Love Deed?


A deed is a legal document that proves the ownership of an asset in the real world. A Love Deed is the evidence of a heart's ownership according to this concept. The rights to the Love Deed belong to the buyer, who therefore owns the heart. New love deeds are issued by Lovereg.com, who acts as the love registry office. It is perfect for all types of relationships and occasions.

All Love Deeds are printed on museum grade art matte paper and printed professionally to give an authentic look and feel.
